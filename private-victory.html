TO WIN THE PRIVATE VICTORY


The daily private victory—time routinely spent each day renewing body, mind, heart, and spirit—is the key to developing all of the 7 Habits. Winning a daily private victory gives us the capacity to win the more difficult challenges played out in the public arenas of our lives.


Brain, Body, Heart, and Spirit are representations of different aspects of your life that require daily renewal.


  • The brain is for intellectual growth and continual learning.

  • The body is for maintaining physical health such as diet and exercise.

  • The heart is for cultivating positive relationships with others.

  • The spirit is for value clarification and commitment, and study and meditation.
